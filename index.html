In 1896,GOLD was discovered in Barstow, CA!

Gold in the desert?

The high desert gold triangle of California produced the most gold for the state, totally 2,461,000 ounces and continues to produce today.

Really, near Barstow, CA?

The Coolgardie Mining District lies right outside of town and miners from 1900-1915 found $9.5 million worth of gold. That's a lot of gold.

How do you mine in the desert?

The two best methods for finding gold in the desert are drywashers & metal detectors. Drywashers use air instead of water.
